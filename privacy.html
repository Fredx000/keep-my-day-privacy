Privacy Policy for Keep My Day

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Keep My Day ("the App," "we," "us") is a personal planner, calendar, and relationship-organizer app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Keep My Day, we collect and store:

  • Account information: your email address and password (used solely for authentication).
  • Content you create, including:
    • Contacts you add (name, phone number, email, address, notes, status, priority)
    • Appointments and calendar events (titles, times, categories, locations)
    • Daily tasks, recurring tasks, and goals you create, along with their progress
    • Settings and preferences you configure within the app

We do not collect your location, browse your contacts/calendar outside the app, or access any device data beyond what you manually enter into the App.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide the App's core functionality: storing and displaying your schedule, contacts, tasks, and goals back to you across your devices. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to anyone.

Data Storage and Third Parties

Your data is stored using Supabase, a backend infrastructure provider, which hosts our database and authentication system. Supabase acts as our data processor and does not use your data for its own purposes.

If you purchase the Pro upgrade, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play (or the Apple App Store, if applicable). We do not receive or store your payment details — we only receive confirmation that a purchase was completed.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) between the App and our servers, and access controls that ensure only you can access your own data.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you would like your account and all associated data permanently deleted, contact us at the email below and we will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Keep My Day is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
