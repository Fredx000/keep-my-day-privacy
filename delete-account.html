Delete Your Keep My Day Account

You can request permanent deletion of your Keep My Day account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

Send an email to arioncaelum19@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Please include the email address you signed up with so we can locate your account.

What gets deleted

Once your request is processed (within 7 days), the following data is permanently and irreversibly deleted:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All contacts you added
  • All appointments and calendar events
  • All daily tasks, recurring tasks, and goals
  • Your app settings and preferences

Data retention

No data is retained after deletion is processed, with the exception of records we are legally required to keep (e.g. for tax/billing purposes related to purchases), which are retained only as long as required by law.

For more information, see our Privacy Policy.
